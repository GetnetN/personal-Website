Research Papers & Articles

Deep Learning Approaches for Plant Disease Detection in Ethiopian Agriculture
Getnet Tigabie Askale, et al.
International Journal of Computer Vision and Applications, 2022

This research explores the application of convolutional neural networks for automated detection and classification of plant diseases in Ethiopian agricultural contexts. The proposed model achieved 94.3% accuracy in identifying common crop diseases from leaf images.

PDF DOI Journal Article
Machine Learning-Based Student Performance Prediction in Higher Education
Getnet Tigabie Askale, et al.
Ethiopian Journal of Education and Technology, 2021

This study investigates the use of machine learning algorithms to predict student academic performance based on demographic, socioeconomic, and academic history data. The research provides insights for early intervention strategies in higher education institutions.

PDF DOI Journal Article
A Framework for Intelligent Educational Systems in Resource-Constrained Environments
Getnet Tigabie Askale
Proceedings of the International Conference on Educational Technology, 2020

This paper presents a framework for developing intelligent educational systems tailored for resource-constrained environments, focusing on adaptive learning pathways and offline functionality to address connectivity challenges in developing regions.
