About Me

Computer Science Lecturer | AI Researcher | Technology Innovator

Professional Journey

I am Getnet Tigabie Askale, a dedicated Computer Science Lecturer at the University of Gondar, Ethiopia, where I have been serving since 2022. My academic career is driven by a strong commitment to teaching excellence, innovative research, and the practical application of computing technologies to address real-world challenges.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe in creating engaging, supportive learning environments that foster independent thinking and problem-solving skills. My approach combines theoretical foundations with practical applications to prepare students for real-world challenges.

Career Vision

To advance AI research while mentoring the next generation of African technology innovators, contributing to both academic excellence and the technological development of Ethiopia and beyond.

Academic Service

Active engagement in curriculum development, student mentorship, departmental initiatives, and scholarly review processes to strengthen academic institutions and support student success.
